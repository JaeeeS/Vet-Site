Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)

MAGNETIC RESONANCE IMAGING. Being the first veterinary facility in the Philippines and the third in Southeast Asia to provide MRI services for animals, we recognize the need of an MRI machine and its implications to progressive veterinary healthcare. This machine is utilized to map and depict visual representations of the nervous systems for both humans and animals. An MRI is considered a current gold standard for nervous system imagery outputs. It provides high-quality images with excellent detail and resolution of the brain and spinal cord, comparatively better than its inferior counterpart being widely used, computed tomography. Being a common diagnostic imaging tool, we believe this is an important machine to advance our veterinary needs. With this machine luckily right at our disposal, we can absolutely formulate quick and more accurate diagnoses beneficial in instances of brain and spinal cord diseases.

HEMODIALYSIS

Hemodialysis is a clinical treatment for acute and in some cases chronic kidney disease. It is a form of blood purification that is used to treat animals with kidney disease or intoxications. During a hemodialysis session, your pet is being closely monitored. The things we keep on a look out for are things such as: Blood work, heart rate, and respiratory rate. Alongside the operation of this machines, we make sure to check your loved ones regularly to make sure they are doing well while receiving extraordinary treatment. We ensure pet health management is holistically considered while being accompanied by the health service our machines can offer.

FLUOROSCOPY

In the field of Fluoroscopy, we offer C-arm Fluoroscopy as our available equipment of choice. C-arm Fluoroscopy is a type of advanced imaging that allows us to view the inner structure and bodily systems of the pet while in locomotor movement. Similar to a radiograph or the well-known X-ray, we ought to capture the insides of your pet while they are in motion. This is done by its intricate procedure of taking multiple shots in rapid frames which depicts the illusion of movement in real time as we sequence them together. This machine can provide a complete evaluation of procedures such as cardiology and swallowing dysfunctions. This important machine allows us to see the abnormalities that occur in motion in order to properly diagnose the problem concerning the pet. One of its most notable uses is to perform dynamic studies, including evaluation for tracheal collapse and swallowing dysfunction. It is also used for interventional cardiology procedures. Because the machine provides a video in motion, we can appreciate, assess, and repeat the functions that we ought to diagnose concerning the animal.

ENDOSCOPY

We added the endoscopy machine in our inventory for an important reason. In general terms, The purpose of this procedure is to help your veterinarian make a diagnosis of the disease that has been causing your pet's clinical signs of vomiting, diarrhea, weight loss, abdominal pain or swelling or loss of appetite. Basically, the usual symptoms that trigger a warning for owners to take their pets to the vet. Endoscopy is a technique that could supplement this need. It is a medical procedure that performs natural openings in the body or through one or more tiny holes. An endoscope inserted in the body magnifies internal structures on a TV monitor for thorough examination. Our endoscopy facility can perform the following procedures: rhinoscopy, bronchoscopy, otoscopy, gastrointestinal endoscopy.
